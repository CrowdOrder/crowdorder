11 Reasons to Hire a Virtual Assistant

  • ben.mcaskell@crowdorder.co.uk
  • 22 Sep 2020

What is a Virtual Assistant?

A Virtual Assistant is a contractor who can help with a variety of services for their clients including but not limited to data entry, market research and customer service. Virtual Assistant’s work from anywhere in the world and can help with the time-consuming tasks that keeps a business owner away from the projects that drive business growth.

Drive Business Growth with a Virtual Assistant

When starting your own business, it can be difficult to do everything yourself of with a small team. Bringing on some extra resource to do deal with the day-to-day tasks that take you away from building the business can be worth its weight in gold.

Access to The Best Talent

You can find some really talented Virtual Assistants out there that can help with a range of tasks. Once a Virtual Assistant understands your business, they can take a lot of the weight off your shoulders.

With your VA being able to work anywhere in the world, you can access a much larger pool of talent and find the best person for your business, regardless of their geographical location.

You can also get some highly competitive prices with our Virtual Assistant partners, so it won’t affect your bottom line as much a full-time employee might do.

After a few months, you will even find that your Virtual Assistant has just become a part of your team.

Decreased Operational Costs

Virtual Assistants can really help keep the costs of running your business down. Use your virtual assistant to cut down on operational expenditures. Using a Virtual Assistant can help you save on office spaces, equipment and even savings on taxes.

Virtual Assistants can also work extremely flexibly so their workloads can be tailored to your business.

Reduced Workload

As an owner of a start up business, you can really get overwhelmed with the huge number of tasks that need to be done to keep the business running and growing. Virtual Assistants can be multi-skilled and can help your business in the following areas:

  • Market Research
  • Data Entry
  • Email Management
  • Online Marketing
  • Scheduling
  • Call Answering
  • Search Engine Optimization
  • Web Development
  • Content Writing
  • Transcription

Reducing the amount of time, you spend on these areas can help you work on more strategic areas of your business.

Focus on The Core Operations

As a business owner, you need to focus on your core business functions. You need to think about what you can’t do yourself and tasks that you need assistance with. These areas are where a great Virtual Assistant can help.

24/7 Availability and Flexible Working

We live in a connected, globalized world so it’s important that your business is always available to customers. Virtual Assistants can work the times that you can’t and ensure your business always has someone on the clock.

It can be a great feeling to know you can switch off on your down time safe in the knowledge that your business is running smoothly.

Billing by the Hour

Virtual Assistants can work by the hour, so you are always getting the best value for money. You don’t need to pay for sick days or vacation meaning you only pay for deliverables that are useful to your business.

Improve Your Online Presence

Many Virtual Assistants are experienced when it comes promoting businesses online.

VA’s can help with:

  • Prompt responses to consumer queries
  • Creation of engaging content
  • Promotional posts and activities
Managing social media profile and creating takes a lot of time and effort and VA can manage your community and ensure your business is front of mind with people who may want to use your services.

Scalability in Operations

As a growing business, you may find that there are times where you need to scale resource quickly and a VA can be a good way to get that extra resource quickly and at a great price.

The additional manpower can help you manage the extra workload and ensure your customers are happy.

Efficient Customer Handling

Customers can ask questions at any time and want issues resolved quickly. VA’s can help with customer service channels such as the website chatbots, telephony and social media.

Give Yourself More Leisure Time

Having a virtual assistant on your side allows you to have more leisure time and switch off from the day-to-day of your business. It’s so important to have a work-life balance and a Virtual Assistant can help you to build your business without affecting your personal life and health.
